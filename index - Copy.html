About Me

The homework instructions tell us to make this about ourselves. I decided to make it about my favorite sports, table tennis. I know some people will scoff table tennis is not a sport. I have just the video for those people.
If you think table tennis is not a sport
If you like to read about some reasons to play table tennis check this page: Why play table tennis?

The following is an excerpt from WikiPedia: Table tennis, also known as ping pong, is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using a small bat. A point is scored when a player fails to return the ball within the rules. Play is fast and demands quick reactions. Spinning the ball alters its trajectory and limits an opponent's options, giving the hitter a great advantage.
